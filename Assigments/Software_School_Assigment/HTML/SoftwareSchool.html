6 steps for dream job

STEP 1

Choose in-demand technology

You need to learn career paths not just basic courses. You need to learn what industry wants. Choose most in-demand technology to land job quickly.

STEP 2

Learn real-time skills not theory

Learning theory won't help you in cracking interviews. Companies want someone who can code not theory specialists. Learn real-time concepts to crack interviews easily.

STEP 3

Practice by building demo apps

Get hands-on experince by building one or two demo projects. You can build blog or ecommerce webiste with minimal features, You can remember most of the things during interview.

STEP 4

GitHub profile to showcase coding skills

Github is a platform for sharing & collbarating. Upload your code to github and share your repositories links along with your resume, so that interviwers will get good understanding about your coding skills.

STEP 5

Resume, LinkedIn & Naukri profiles

Build best resume with github profile links, projects links and upload it on naukri. LinkedIn is the best platform for professionals. Create your profile and get in touch with recruiters who are actively hiring.

STEP 6

Interview prep & mock interviews

Interview preparation and mock interviews are the most important things before attending any interview. Go through the frequently asked asked questions and practive two or more mock interviews.
